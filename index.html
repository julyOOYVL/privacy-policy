This Privacy Policy explains how P-Pilot handles your information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this document.

✈   POLICY SECTIONS   ✈
01

Information We Collect

  • Pilot profiles, optional profile images, and app settings
  • Flight log entries — dates, routes, aircraft info, day/night hours
  • Premium and trial access status stored on device
  • Device features accessed: photo library or file picker for profile images or backup import/export
  • Local storage access for PDF or JSON file generation
02

How We Use Your Information

  • Store and display your flight records and logbook data
  • Calculate monthly totals and generate PDF/report exports
  • Manage your preferences and premium access status
  • Import or restore backup files when requested
03

Cloud Services

The App currently stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit it to any external server or cloud service.

⚠️

Future cloud features may be added. If so, this Privacy Policy will be updated and you will be notified before any data leaves your device.

04

Payments

  • Premium access is available via in-app purchases through the Apple App Store or Google Play
  • The App does not collect payment card details directly
  • All purchases are processed by the respective platform provider (Apple / Google)
  • Your premium status is stored locally on your device, but eligible purchases can be restored on a new device through the same Apple App Store or Google Play account
05

Data Sharing

🔒

We do not sell your information. Your data is only shared if you explicitly export files yourself, or if we are legally required to do so.

06

Analytics & Advertising

✅

P-Pilot contains no advertisements and does not include any third-party analytics or tracking SDK.

07

Data Retention & Security

  • Data is retained locally until you delete it, uninstall the App, or overwrite it with a new import
  • We use secure device storage mechanisms provided by the OS
  • You are responsible for securing your device, backups, and exported files
08

Your Controls

  • Edit or delete pilot profiles and individual flight entries at any time
  • Export, import, or share files at your own discretion
  • Manage device permissions (photos, storage) in system settings
  • Uninstall the App at any time to delete all locally stored data
09

Children

P-Pilot is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum required age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

10

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of material changes within the App or through the applicable store listing. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
